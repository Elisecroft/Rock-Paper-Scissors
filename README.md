## How to play

### Rules 

You play against the CPU. Choose an icon. The first with 3 points win.

-   Rock 💎 beat scissors ✂️
-   Scissors ✂️ beat paper 🧻
-   Paper 🧻 beat rock 💎

### Where

You can play at this link : https://elisecroft.github.io/Rock-Paper-Scissors/
